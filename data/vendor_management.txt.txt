CloudNest AI - Vendor Management Policy

Vendor Assessment:
All vendors undergo security assessments.

Risk Classification:
Vendors are categorized by risk level.

Access Control:
Vendor access is limited to business requirements.

Periodic Review:
Vendor security reviews occur annually.

Third-Party Monitoring:
Critical vendors are continuously monitored.

Vendor Offboarding:
Vendor access is revoked immediately after contract termination.
