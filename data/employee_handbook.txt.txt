CloudNest AI - Employee Handbook

Company Overview:
CloudNest AI is a SaaS startup with 25 employees.

Code of Conduct:
Employees must maintain professionalism and confidentiality.

Password Policy:
Passwords must be at least 12 characters long.
Passwords expire every 12 months.

Multi-Factor Authentication:
MFA is required for all employee accounts.

Employee Onboarding:
New employees receive security training and account provisioning.

Employee Offboarding:
Employee access must be revoked within 24 hours of termination.

Remote Work Policy:
Remote work requires manager approval.

Device Policy:
Company laptops must be encrypted and regularly updated.
