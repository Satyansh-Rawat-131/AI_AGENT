CloudNest AI - Security Policy

Access Control:
Access is granted according to business requirements.

Least Privilege:
Employees only receive access necessary for their job roles.

Authentication:
Strong passwords and MFA are required.

Encryption:
Sensitive data is encrypted at rest and in transit.

Logging:
Security logs are collected and retained.

Monitoring:
Systems are continuously monitored for suspicious activity.

Vulnerability Management:
Security patches are applied monthly.
